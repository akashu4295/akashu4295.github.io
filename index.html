About

I am currently pursuing my Ph.D. at the Indian Institute of Technology Gandhinagar, India, where my research delves into the fascinating world of shear flows within intricate enclosures. More specifically, my work is centered around investigating the complex phenomena of bifurcations in Taylor-Couette flow within these intricate enclosures. This research is not only intellectually stimulating but also has significant practical implications, particularly in the context of fluid dynamics and understanding the behavior of fluids in confined spaces. By exploring the intricate dynamics of these shear flows, I aim to contribute valuable insights to the field, with potential applications ranging from industrial processes to environmental modeling. I am passionate about unraveling the mysteries of fluid mechanics and excited about the opportunities to expand our knowledge in this domain.
